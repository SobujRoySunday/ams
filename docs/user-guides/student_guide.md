# Student Guide

## Login & Dashboard

1. Go to [AMS Login Page](https://yourdomain.com/login)
2. Log in using your student credentials or Google account.

## View Attendance

- Navigate to **Dashboard > Attendance**.
- View your subject-wise attendance summary.

## Elective Courses

- Navigate to **Electives** section.
- Choose preferred electives and submit.

---

### Tips

- Use Google Chrome for best compatibility.
- For issues, contact your department admin.
